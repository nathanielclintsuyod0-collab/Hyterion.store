  • ➡ NO GRIEFING: Do not destroy, modify, or interfere with other players' builds without permission.
  • ➡ NO STEALING: Respect all chests and storage. Do not take items that aren't yours.
  • ➡ BE RESPECTFUL: No harassment, bullying, racism, or excessive trash talk in chat. Keep it friendly like a peaceful biome.
  • ➡ NO HACKING/CHEATING: The use of X-Ray, hacked clients (Toolbox, Wurst, Meteor), exploits, or item duplication is strictly prohibited.
  • ➡ RESPECT STAFF: Always follow instructions from developer, admins, moderators and server owners.

# : COMMUNITY RULES

  • ➡ SPAWN PROTECTION: Building is not allowed near spawn. Keep the area clean and accessible.
  • ➡ BUILD DISTANCE: Maintain a reasonable distance from other players and spawn to avoid overcrowding.
  • ➡ FIX ENVIRONMENTAL DAMAGE: Clean up after yourself. Repair any damage you cause to terrain or structures.
  • ➡ NO LAG MACHINES: Any builds or contraptions that cause server lag are not allowed.
  • ➡ NO SILVERFISH SPAWNER BUILDS: Creating or abusing Silverfish spawners/farms that may cause lag or disrupt gameplay is strictly prohibited.
